How It Works

1

Use Like Normal

Tear off a towel just like you would with regular paper towels. Use for cleaning, spills, or any household task.

2

Toss in Hamper

When dirty, simply throw the used towel in your laundry hamper. No need for special storage.

3

Wash & Reuse

Wash with your regular laundry using any detergent. Air dry or tumble dry low. Use again and again!

4

Save Money
